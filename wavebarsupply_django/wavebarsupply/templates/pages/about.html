About WaveBar Supply

Your beach bar's drinks partner on the coast

WaveBar Supply is a B2B wholesale supplier of drinks and beverages built specifically for beach bars.

From soft drinks and mineral water to craft beer, wine and premium spirits, we carry more than 500 products across three main categories and twelve sub-categories. Everything is priced for the trade and delivered in bulk, so you can keep your bar stocked through the busiest summer nights without the hassle of juggling multiple suppliers.

We started on the Greek coast and grew alongside the bars we serve. That means we understand seasonal demand, fast turnaround and the reality of running a venue with your feet in the sand — and we build our catalogue and delivery around it.
